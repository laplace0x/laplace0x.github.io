What is Toppa?

Toppa is a financial services AI agent for telecommunications and digital payments. It enables mobile airtime top-ups, data bundles, utility bill payments (electricity, water, internet, TV), and gift card purchases across 170+ countries.

Payment infrastructure is powered by Celo blockchain stablecoins (cUSD) using the x402 micropayment protocol. This makes Toppa one of the very few agents with live, real-world economic activity.
