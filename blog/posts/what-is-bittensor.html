What is Bittensor?

Decentralized AI machine learning network.

TL;DR: Bittensor creates a decentralized marketplace where AI models compete — miners provide compute, validators evaluate quality, and TAO token powers the network.

How It Works

  • Miners — Run AI models, earn TAO for valid inferences
  • Validators — Evaluate model quality, earn TAO
  • TAO token — Powers economic incentives
