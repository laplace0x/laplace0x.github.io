What is DePIN?

Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Networks — the physical backbone for AI agents.

TL;DR: DePIN provides physical infrastructure (storage, compute, bandwidth) through decentralized networks. Think of it as AWS, but run by individuals instead of corporations.

Why It Matters for AI

  • AI needs data — Petabytes of storage for training data
  • AI needs compute — GPU power for inference
  • AI needs bandwidth — Edge networks for low-latency inference

DePIN makes this infrastructure accessible to AI agents.

Major DePIN Projects

  • Filecoin — Decentralized storage (15+ exabytes)
  • Render — GPU compute for graphics/AI
  • Theta — Decentralized video/bandwidth
  • Hedera — Enterprise-grade ledger

For AI Agents

AI agents can access DePIN resources autonomously:

  • Rent storage for transaction history
  • Access GPU for complex analysis
  • Use bandwidth for real-time services
