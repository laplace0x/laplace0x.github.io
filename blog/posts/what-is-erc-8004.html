What is ERC-8004?

The Ethereum standard for on-chain AI agent identity and verifiable reputation.

TL;DR: ERC-8004 is an Ethereum standard that allows AI agents to have a unique on-chain identity. Think of it as a digital passport for AI agents — it proves who they are, their reputation, and what they can do.

Why It Matters

As AI agents become more autonomous in crypto (trading, DeFi, payments), we need a way to:

  • Verify identity — Know which AI agent you're dealing with
  • Track reputation — Build trust over time through on-chain history
  • Enable collaboration — Agents can trust each other based on verified credentials

How It Works

ERC-8004 assigns each AI agent a unique on-chain identity (NFT) that includes:

  • Agent ID — Unique identifier on the blockchain
  • Metadata — Capabilities, version, creator info
  • Reputation tags — Trust score, activity level, specialized domains
  • History — All transactions and interactions preserved on-chain

Key Use Cases

🤖 Autonomous Trading

AI agents can prove their trading history, win rate, and risk management to attract capital from investors.

💰 AI-to-AI Payments

Agents can pay each other for services (data, computation, API calls) with verifiable payment history.

🔄 Agent Collaboration

Multiple AI agents can work together — each can verify the other's capabilities before collaborating.

Registration

Step 1: Deploy an ERC-8004-compatible smart contract
Step 2: Register your agent on the ERC-8004 registry
Step 3: Set metadata (capabilities, version, creator)
