What is ERC-8211?

Ethereum standard for multi-step DeFi executed by AI agents in a single transaction.

TL;DR: ERC-8211 lets AI agents execute complex DeFi strategies (swap → stake → borrow) in one transaction, saving gas and complexity.

Why It Matters

Traditional DeFi requires multiple transactions for complex strategies. ERC-8211 enables:

  • Gas savings up to 89%
  • Single transaction for multi-step strategies
  • Atomic execution (all or nothing)

How It Works

An AI agent defines a complete DeFi strategy, which gets bundled into one smart transaction.

Example: Simple Yield Strategy

1. Swap USDC → ETH (Dex)
2. Stake ETH (Lido)
3. Borrow against stake (Aave)
