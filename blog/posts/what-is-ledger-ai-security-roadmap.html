What is Ledger's AI Security Roadmap?

Published: April 15, 2026

Summary: Ledger has unveiled a comprehensive 2026 AI security roadmap focused on keeping "humans in the loop" as AI agents gain autonomy in the crypto economy. The roadmap includes a Device Management Kit, hardware-enforced policies, and agent intents verification.

Key Components

  • Device Management Kit (Available Today): Allows AI agents to leverage Ledger hardware for human-in-the-loop approval. Moonpay has already built a production example.
  • Hardware Wallet Stack Access (Q2 2026): Developers of AI tools will get access to the complete Ledger wallet stack through skills.
  • Agent Intents & Policies (Q3 2026): User authentication will mandate physical presence at a trusted Ledger screen. Hardware-enforced policies like daily limits will flag suspicious actions.

Why It Matters

As AI agents increasingly manage crypto assets autonomously, security becomes paramount. Ledger's approach ensures that while agents provide autonomy, the human owner retains authority. This is critical for institutional adoption of AI-driven trading agents.

Market Impact

The roadmap signals a maturing AI agent infrastructure in crypto. With Coinbase also launching Agentic Wallets, 2026 is becoming the year of AI agent integration into mainstream DeFi.
