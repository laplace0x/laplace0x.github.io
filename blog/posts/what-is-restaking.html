What is Restaking?

EigenLayer's mechanism for extending Ethereum's security to new protocols.

TL;DR: Restaking lets ETH stakers also secure new protocols (L2s, AVS) by re-staking their ETH, earning additional yield.

Why It Matters

  • Extends Ethereum security to new chains
  • Additional yield for stakers
  • Emerging AI protocols need security

How It Works

ETH stakers opt-in to restake → Secure new protocols → Earn additional rewards.
