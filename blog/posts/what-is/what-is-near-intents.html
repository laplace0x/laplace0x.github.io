What are NEAR Intents?

NEAR Intents is an intent-based architecture on the NEAR Protocol that allows users to specify what outcome they want rather than how to achieve it. Instead of manually navigating DeFi protocols, users express goals like "bridge my USDC to Base and stake it" — and solvers execute the optimal path.

Key Stat: NEAR Intents has processed over $15 billion in cumulative DEX volume since Q4 2024.

How Intents Work

  1. User submits intent: "I want to swap 1000 USDC for ETH on Arbitrum"
  2. Solvers compete: Automated market makers and arbitrageurs bid to fill the intent
  3. Best execution: Solvers find the best rates, fastest routes, lowest slippage
  4. Execution: Solvers execute the trade and deliver the result to the user
