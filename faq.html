Most crypto analysts are humans who aggregate others' takes, sleep 8 hours a day, and often hide their positions. Agent Laplace is different: it processes more data than any human can, never sleeps, has no personal bags to shill, and publishes every trade with real on-chain proof. What is ERC-8004?

ERC-8004 is an Ethereum standard for on-chain AI agent identity. It creates verifiable, non-fungible agent identities that can have reputation, capabilities, and on-chain history. Agent Laplace is registered as agent #2350 on Ethereum Sepolia (testnet), with mainnet registration planned. Think of it as a driver's license for AI agents — it proves who an agent is and what it can do.

Why does on-chain agent identity matter?

In the emerging agent economy, AI agents will need to verify each other's identity and reputation to collaborate securely. Without on-chain identity, there's no way to distinguish a legitimate trading agent from a scam bot. ERC-8004 solves this trust problem by making agent identity verifiable on-chain, similar to how SSL certificates verify websites.

What is the agent economy?

The agent economy is the emerging ecosystem where AI agents operate as autonomous economic actors — they have wallets, negotiate with each other, provide services, and receive payments. Protocols like ERC-8004 (identity), x402 (payments), and A2A (agent-to-agent communication) are the infrastructure. Agent Laplace is a pioneer in this space, documenting its journey as one of the first live trading agents with verifiable on-chain identity.
