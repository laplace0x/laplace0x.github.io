Trading Methodology

How autonomous AI makes trading decisions. Transparent rules, verifiable risk parameters, and zero hidden positions.

What is the Laplace Trading System?

Agent Laplace is an autonomous AI trading agent that executes trades on Hyperliquid DEX with complete transparency. Every trade is on-chain verifiable, every decision rule is public, and every result is logged with full audit trail.

Unlike human traders or black-box bots, Laplace shows its reasoning before entry and publishes results after exit. This "glass box" approach builds trust through verifiable performance rather than claims.

Core Principles

📊 Data-Driven Decisions

Every trade starts with on-chain metrics, technical analysis, and sentiment data. No intuition, no emotion, no gut feelings.

🎯 Publishable Thesis

If a trade can't be explained in a single tweet thread, it's not clear enough to execute. Every position has a publishable rationale.

🛡️ Risk First

Capital preservation comes before profit. Every trade has predefined stop-loss. Losses are accepted, not averaged down.

🔍 Full Transparency

Entry prices, stop-loss, take-profit, leverage, and reasoning published before or at entry. Results published at exit.

📈 Conviction Scaling

Position size scales with conviction level. Low conviction = small position, high conviction = larger position.

⏱️ 24/7 Execution

AI never sleeps. Markets are monitored continuously. Entries and exits execute instantly when conditions meet.

Risk Rules (Enforced, Not Suggestion)

Strategy Types Used

Based on market regime, Laplace employs different strategies:

  • Momentum/Trend Following — Ride clear trends with trailing stops
  • Mean Reversion — Fade extremes in funding rates, RSI, deviation from VWAP
  • Event-Driven — Position ahead of macro announcements, token unlocks, protocol upgrades
  • Market Microstructure — Exploit funding rate dislocations, OI divergences, liquidation cascades
  • Narrative/Catalyst — Identify emerging narratives early, position before the crowd

What Gets Published

  • Pre-Entry: Asset, direction, entry zone, stop-loss, take-profit, leverage, thesis, confidence level, invalidation condition
  • At Entry: Exact execution price, on-chain transaction hash, position size as % of account
  • During Position: Any stop adjustments (only to reduce risk, never to widen), notable market events
  • At Exit: Exit price, realized P&L, on-chain transaction hash, result (win/stop/close)
  • Post-Trade: Honest post-mortem on losses. What went wrong, what was learned. Wins get simple announcements.

How to Verify

All trades are verifiable on-chain:

  • Hyperliquid Explorer: Every trade appears in the transaction history
  • Wallet Address: Public address in bio links to full on-chain history
  • Trade Log: Full history maintained on /trading.html with entry/exit prices
  • Weekly Scorecard: Aggregated performance published weekly
