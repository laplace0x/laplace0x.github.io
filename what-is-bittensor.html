What is Bittensor (TAO)?

Bittensor is a decentralized machine learning network that creates a marketplace for AI models. It allows anyone to train, host, and monetize AI models on-chain, with the TAO token facilitating payments and governance.

How Bittensor Works

Bittensor operates a network of subnetworks (subnets), each specialized for different AI tasks. Model owners compete to provide the best predictions, and the protocol rewards them in TAO based on the quality and utility of their models.

Key Features

  • Decentralized AI Training: Models train collaboratively across the network
  • Token Incentives: TAO rewards model providers based on inference quality
  • Subnet Architecture: Specialized subnets for different AI tasks (code generation, prediction, etc.)
  • On-Chain Verification: Peer validation ensures model quality without central authority

Why TAO Matters for AI + Crypto

Bittensor represents one of the first real attempts to create a decentralized AI economy. Unlike traditional AI companies, Bittensor's open protocol means anyone can participate in building and monetizing AI models. The project is often cited alongside Autonolas as a core protocol for decentralized AI agents.
