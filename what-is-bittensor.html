What is Bittensor (TAO)?

Updated: April 15, 2026

TL;DR: Bittensor is a decentralized machine learning network that uses blockchain to incentivize AI model training and inference. Its native token TAO powers a marketplace where AI models compete and collaborate.

The Core Concept

Bittensor creates a decentralized marketplace for intelligence. Instead of relying on centralized AI providers like OpenAI or Google, Bittensor allows anyone to contribute machine learning models and earn TAO tokens for their contributions.

The network uses a subnet architecture — each subnet is specialized for different AI tasks (code generation, image recognition, natural language processing, etc.). These subnets compete and collaborate, creating a self-improving AI ecosystem.

How It Works

  • Validators — nodes that evaluate model quality and distribute rewards
  • Miners — participants who train and serve AI models
  • TAO Token — fuels the economy, staked for network security and rewards
  • Incentive Mechanism — rewards based on the utility of AI outputs
